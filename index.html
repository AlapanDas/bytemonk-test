What is ByteMonk?

ByteMonk is the Technical Club of Government College of Engineering and Ceramic Technology, established in 2020. We Aim To Educate The GCECT Community On The Latest Technology Trends And Skills, To Facilitate Recruiting Opportunities And To Foster Social And Professional Networking Within GCECT And Beyond

What is GDSC?

GDSC GCECT is the Google Developer Student Club of Government College of Engineering and Ceramic Technology, established in 2020. We Aim To Educate The GCECT Community On The Latest Google Technology, Trends And Skills. We also provide various programs to engage them about G-Technlogies
